Quiche recipe

Ingredients:
Shortcrust pastry (1 sheet)
3 eggs
125g ham or bacon bits
1 cup grated tasty cheese
300ml thickened cream
salt
pepper
nutmeg
cayenne

Step 1.
Pre-heat oven to 180 degrees celsius (160 degrees fan forced). Lightly grease quiche dish and line with sheet of pastry. Cut corners and patch as appropriate until dish completely lined.

Step 2.
Beat eggs lightly with fork. Add cream and stir to combine.

Step 3.
Add salt, pepper, nutmeg and cayenne to egg and cream mixture (to taste). Stir to combine.

Step 4. 
Spread bacon/ham evenly in pastry dish. Sprinkle cheese evenly on top.

Step 5.
Pour egg and cream mixture over bacon/ham and cheese in pastry dish.

Step 6.
Place in oven and bake for approx. 30 mins until starting to brown on top. Leave to cool for 10 minutes before serving.
